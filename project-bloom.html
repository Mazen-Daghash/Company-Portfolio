Project Overview

Transforming a floral boutique into a modern, sustainable brand

Bloom & Petal is a boutique floral design studio that specializes in sustainable, locally-sourced floral arrangements. They approached us to create a brand identity that reflects their commitment to eco-friendly practices and modern, minimalist design.

Our challenge was to develop a visual language that communicates both the natural beauty of their products and their contemporary, design-forward approach to floral arrangements.

Key Highlights

  • Created a distinctive brand identity system
  • Designed a responsive e-commerce website
  • Developed custom illustrations and patterns
  • Established brand guidelines for consistency

Key Features

Organic Design

Hand-drawn floral elements and flowing typography that captures the natural beauty of their products.

Sustainable Materials

Eco-friendly packaging solutions that align with their commitment to environmental responsibility.

Color Palette

Soft, nature-inspired colors that evoke freshness, growth, and natural beauty.

The Results

+120% Brand Recognition
+90% Customer Engagement
98% Client Satisfaction

The new brand identity successfully positioned Bloom & Petal as a leader in sustainable floral design, resulting in increased customer loyalty and market share. The cohesive visual system has been implemented across all touchpoints, from their website to their physical storefront and packaging.
